Green Garden Box from Recycled Materials

Back to projects...

Project: Reuse Design Challenge (The Greenbox)
Course: Environmental Sustainability
Duration: 9 days (August 23 2021 - August 31 2021)
Objective: Designing a replicable product made of recycled materials to benefit the environment and the user
Type of project: Group project (2 partners)
Documentation...

In this project, me and my partners, Bella and Hayden, worked on researching, designing, and making a prototype of a system that could have real use to the user and keep those materials from ending up in a landfill or the ocean. I proposed the idea of having the project be a gardening bed made out of an old cardboard box I had at home, and we all agreed on the Idea. Hayden worked on the slides, and Bella and I worked on refining the design to take multiple considerations into account, such as including a plastic layering on the inside layer to prevent water from leaking into the cardboard, adding an openable plastic layer as a roof to protect against rain, and adding a cardboard tube to allow water to flow into the bottom. If we had more time to work on the project, as well as more resources and real funding, we would make the project into a wicking bed, which uses capillary action to distribute the water.
